About EcoWare Solutions

EcoWare Solutions was founded in 2015 with a simple mission — to replace single-use plastic tableware with planet-friendly alternatives. We work with businesses of all sizes, from local restaurants to global hotel chains.

Our manufacturing unit follows strict environmental standards. Every product we make is tested for quality, safety, and sustainability before it reaches your hands. We believe good business and good environment go hand in hand.

With a team of 200+ professionals and a presence in 50+ countries, we are committed to making sustainable choices accessible to every business.

🎯 Our Mission: "To make every meal eco-friendly by providing the world's best biodegradable tableware at the most competitive prices."
